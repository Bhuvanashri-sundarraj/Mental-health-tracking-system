Tips To Prevent Sexual Harassment

  • Pay attention to your boundaries : Never allow anyone to cross your fixed physical boundaries unless you fully believe them and feel comfortable around them.
  • Avoid addressing improper questions : Keep discussion about another person's intent out of it when addressing unwanted communication or contact.
  • Explain to them : If the person is close to you explain your pain and worries.
  • Reach for help : Feel free to reach for the people around whom you feel comfortable.
  • Never blame you : Remember you are never responsible for the other person's behaviour.
  • Loop through songs : Listen to motivational enthusiastic songs that vibe you up.
  • Try to socialise : Staying in a room alone can make you always remember the same things so try joining a club or a class for self help.
  • Note down your feelings : Make entry of your feelings in a notebook and keep checking if your efforts help you improve.
  • Try counselling : If you are desperate for help reach out for a counseller or psychologist.
