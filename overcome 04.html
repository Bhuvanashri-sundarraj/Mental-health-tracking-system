Tips To Overcome Exam Stress





Tips Overcome Exam Stress :


It's time to get moving !

  • Stretch : Stretching reduces tension and blood pressure.
  • Take a short walk : Walking gives you time to think and plan your schedule with a free mind.
  • Exercise regularly : Do simple,enjoyable form of exercise with music or audio podcasts.
  • Get some sunlight : 5 to 15 minutes of sunlight increases your serotonin level
  • Get enough sleep : Irreguar sleeping patterns can increase your stress level
  • Do breathing exercises : Try belly breathing and morning breathing techniques
  • Get enough vitamin C : Vitamin C reduces blood pressure and cortisol which are signs of stress.
      You can eat,
    1. Guava
    2. Pepper/Capsicum
    3. Strawberries
    4. Orange
    5. Tomato
  • Reduce sugar intake : Cortisol also affects your blood sugar level, so the more your sugar intake spikes, the more stressed you’ll feel.
      Do's
    1. Eat a high protien breakfast with eggs and nuts
    2. Eat 4 to 5 servings of fruit and vegetables a day
    3. Eat more fish

      Dont's
    1. Skip breakfast
    2. Eat sugary cereals or candy
    3. Drink sugary drinks

Some organisation is needed too !

  • Clear your room and desk : A cluttered desk is a sign of clutterd mind
  • Learn and apply time management techniques : Managing time helps you avoid feeling overwhelmed so reduces stress.
    1. Follow your proper sleep pattern
    2. Take a break after every 50 minutes of studying
    3. Block out time for studying
  • Don't multitask : It increases heart rate and blood pressure and causes stress.
      To avoid multitasking,
    1. Get rid of distractions
    2. Close all unused tabs in computer screen
    3. List out a to-do list
    4. Set realistic deadline for every task on the list
  • Reduce your phone usage :
    1. Check social media once or twice a day
    2. Turn off all notifications
    3. Put your phone on aeroplane mode or better switch off during study hours

Stimulate your senses and De-stress,

 Sing your heart out               Read something for leisure                      Try aromatherapy(Lavender, Lemon, Jasmine)


 Drink Black Tea                    Eat dark chocolate                                    Listen to inspirational podcasts

Write positive things about you
Journaling is one of the most popular and effective forms of positive writing. It can help you process your thoughts and emotions, gain clarity and perspective, and improve your overall well-being.
Think positove
Positive thinking can have a range of physical and mental health benefits. Building a few habits, like positive self-talk and journaling, can help you get started.
