Tips To Overcome the Feeling Of Guilt

  • Acknowledge it exists : Sometimes guilt can remain hidden underneath other symptoms such as anxiety or sleeplessness.Find th root cause of your problem.
  • Eliminate negative self-talk : Although you did something that is less than ideal, it doesnt define who you are.
  • Remind yourself of all that you do : Consider making a list all positive things you do to outweigh any percieved transgressions.
  • Realize its ok to have needs : No one can be everything to everybody at all times.
  • Establish boundaries : Make your expectations clear and accept others boundaries.
  • Make amends : Try apologising or speak your thoughts out to a person you feel you have harmed to which your guiltiness relates to.
  • Understand what you can control : If the source of guilt is not that you can control then dont worry about it.
  • Address any mental health challenges : If past trauma plays a role in your guilt then try talking to a therapist.
  • Acknowledge that perfection doesn't exist : No one is perfect and everyone make mistakes. We must learn from it.
