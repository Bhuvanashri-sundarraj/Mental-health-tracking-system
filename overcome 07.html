Tips To Overcome Loneliness

  • Practice self-kindness : Blaming ourselves when we feel lonely is not helpful. Take a walk in nature or a day at spa.
  • Enjoy the present moment : If you experience something happy then share with a friend through text or call.
  • Connect in real life : We build stronger in-person connections by looking into their eyes,listening and choosing them over technologies.
  • Rethink how you spend your spare time : Try avoiding using phones too often and face the people while in a social gathering to increase bonding.
  • Do more things with people : Face to face interactions improve our mood and reduce depression.
  • Talk to stranger : Trivial interactions with strangers like cashiers or baristas is able to keep loneliness at bay by helping us more socially connected.
  • Be active online : Instead of simply surfing through the internet try playing group online games or start a group chat to interact with others.
  • Stop your negative thought cycles : Thinking abou our loneliness does no good instead try planning schedule for gym or club sessions to divert your thoughts.
  • Generate a sense of awe : Awe makes time seem like its standing still helps us be more open to connecting.
  • Spend money on experiences : Spending money for getting new experiences is worth for increasing our mental health.
  • Pay to the things that matter : Start paying attention to the things that really matter and leave the rest that is not even important to worry about.
