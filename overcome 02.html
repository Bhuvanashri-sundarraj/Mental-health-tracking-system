Tips To Fight Anxiety








Tips To Fight Anxiety

  • Question Your Thought Pattern : Ask if your fears are worth the worry
  • Practice Focussed,Deep Breathing : This lowers your heart rate thus calming you
  • Use Aromatherapy : Sit near an aromatic item that soothens your mind
  • Exercise : Focus on your body to relax your mind
  • Grounding Techniques : Write down the thoughts to make them less daunting.

Strategies to cope up with anxiety

Learn to Identify & Manage your Triggers
Triggers may be due to work/relationship stress, Withdrawal from drugs, Memory of past trauma, Caffeine and Smoking
Cognitive Behaviourial Therapy
Try self talking with yourself to find solutions to your recurring thoughts.
Try Meditation Practice
When done regularly helps your mind to manage your anxious thoughts. Increases positive thoughts. If sitting still is hard, start with walking meditaion or yoga.
Try Writing Journals
Paper has more patience than people ! Paper doesnt judge you so be free and true when you express your feeling in the paper.
Socialize
Spending friends and fam might help you overcome social anxiety. Socialization helps relieve stress, encourage feelings of laughter and togetherness.
Stay Active
Regualr exercise have proven to reduce anxiety by giving you a good sleep !
Diet & Supplements
Supplements like Lemon balm, Omega-3 fatty acids, Ashwagandha, Green tea, Valerian root and Kava kava have proven to reduce the effects of anxiety !
Place ice cubes on Palm
Keeping ice cubes on palm have been proven to reduce your panic feelings
Keep Stock of Sour Candies
Sour candies reduce the amount of panic feeling in your body and help you relax!
