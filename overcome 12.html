Tips To Overcome Your Fears





Most Common Phobias

  • Claustrophobia — fear of enclosed spaces
  • Necrophobia or thanatophobia — fear of death
  • Cynophobia — fear of dogs
  • Agoraphobia — fear of open places or fear of leaving the house.
  • Metathesiophobia — fear of change
  • Stage Fright - Fear which occurs when an individual is faced with the requirement of performing in frint of an audience.
  • Anthropophobia - Fear of people or society
  • Atelophobia - Fear of imperfection
  • Atychiphobia - Fear of failure
  • Autophobia - Fear of being alone
  • Catagelophobia - Fear of being ridiculed
  • Decidophobia - Fear of making decisions
  • Glossophobia - Fear of speaking in public
  • Nomophobia - Fear of being without your mobile phone
  • Obesophobia - Fear of gaining weight
  • Scoptophobia - Fear of being stared at
  • Sociophobia - Fear of social evaluation

Symptoms of phobias

  1. Chest tightness or pain
  2. Chills or hot flashes
  3. Choking sensations
  4. Confusion
  5. Difficulty breathing
  6. Dizziness
  7. Dry mouth
  8. Increased blood pressure
  9. Nausea
  10. Racing heartbeat
  11. Shaking or trembling
  12. Sweating
