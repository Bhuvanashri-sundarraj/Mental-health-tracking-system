Three Feet From Gold

Chapter 1: The Frustrated Miner

A man had been mining in Colorado for months, but had not struck gold yet.
Frustrated, he quit his job and sold his equipment to another miner.

The new miner was advised by his engineer that there was gold only three feet away from where the first miner had stopped digging.

Chapter 2: The Engineer's Revelation

The engineer was right - the first miner had been mere feet away from striking gold before he gave up and quit.

Chapter 3: The Lesson Learned

The first miner's premature departure meant he missed out on the gold that was just within reach.
His story serves as a cautionary tale about perseverance in the face of adversity.
