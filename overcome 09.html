Tips To Overcome Extreme Introvertness





Tips For Stop being an Introvert

  • Start with small talk : Engage in light chit-chat with people you see regularly, like neighbors or coworkers. The more you do it, the more comfortable it will feel.
  • Push your comfort zone : Face your discomforts to help you build your confidence over time. Strike up a chat with a person next to you in a line or a gathering.
  • Listen and ask questions : Show interest in others by listening to what they share and asking follow-up questions.
  • Focus outward, not inward : Make an effort to focus outward toward the people you're engaging with.
  • Start with "yes" : Challenge yourself to say yes more often to join social gathering to avoid being left alone from trips and tours.
  • Prepare conversation topics in advance : If you know the topics in advance break the awkward silence by the questions you already prepared.
  • Focus on listening, not talking : We dont have to be the life of the party or keep a conversation going. Listen and people will appreciate your genuineness.
